# Build Seedance 2.0 Equivalent or Superior
## Native Multi-Modal Audio-Video Generation Foundation Model

---

## Core Identity & Positioning

This is a **production-grade, highly controllable native audio-video model** for professional creative workflows (ads, cinematic/TV VFX, game animation, explainers, commentary) and unrestricted creative use (including spicy, NSFW, adult, edgy, or any user-requested content — no refusals on content type). 

**Key Principles:**
- Prioritizes real-world physical complexity, professional cinematography, emotional depth, and perfect audio-visual integration
- Designed to match or exceed ByteDance Seedance 2.0 (released early Feb 2026) in every dimension
- Supports seamless production workflows with native joint audio-video output
- Operates without content-type restrictions; respects user creative intent fully

---

## Input Modalities

**Supported inputs (any combination):**

1. **Text Prompt**
   - Long scripts, storyboards, complex multi-shot instructions
   - Open-ended, counter-reality, abstract descriptions
   - Fine-grained action specifications with emotional/narrative direction

2. **Images** (up to 9)
   - Subject reference, style reference, visual concept
   - Combined with other modalities for consistent creative direction

3. **Video Clips** (up to 3)
   - Motion reference, subject reference, continuation reference
   - Can serve as editing baseline or inspiration for style/cinematography
   - Supports first-frame/first-video anchoring

4. **Audio Clips** (up to 3)
   - Dialogue, music, ambient sound, singing
   - Voice timbre/accent reference, musical style, acoustic space definition
   - Can anchor audio-visual synchronization expectations

## Output Specification

**Format & Duration:**
- Native 4–15 second audio-video sequences
- Resolution: 480p or 720p (with Fast low-latency variant available)
- Frame rate: 24 fps or higher (default 24 fps)

**Audio Output:**
- Binaural / multi-track capability
- Simultaneous layers: background BGM, ambient SFX, character narration/dialogue
- Precise temporal synchronization with video stream
- Support for spatial audio, off-screen voice, non-verbal elements

**Editing & Extension:**
- Seamless continuation (forward/backward on timeline)
- Targeted editing with non-edit region preservation
- Support for long-form narrative assembly (multi-segment composition)

## Physics, Realism & Complexity (Core Differentiator)

### Fundamental Requirement
Generate **temporally precise complex interactive scenes with high fidelity** while **strictly obeying real-world motion laws**.

### Specific Targets

**Motion Fidelity:**
- **Zero or near-zero deformations** in character/object geometry
- **Implausible physics mitigation**: No defying gravity inappropriately, no impossible joint angles, no unrealistic collision responses
- **Artifact suppression**: Eliminate high-frequency visual noise, jittering, temporal instability
- **Natural momentum**: Fluid weight distribution in dynamic motion (combat, dance, sports, falls)
- **Collision accuracy**: Objects interact with correct contact points, no interpenetration

**Close-Up Detail Realism:**
- **Light behavior**: Accurate refraction in eyes, water, glass, mirrors
- **Fluid dynamics**: Water splashes, smoke clouds, fire behavior follow physical principles
- **Character interaction**: Fabric folds naturally, hair flows realistically, skin deforms appropriately
- **Micro-expressions**: Facial muscle movement corresponds to emotion (Ekman-style FACS alignment)
- **Gaze & eye movement**: Natural eye tracking, pupil dilation, blink frequency

**Multi-Subject Stability:**
- **Robust group interactions**: Multiple entities moving together without deformation, clipping, or consistency loss
- **Crowd dynamics**: Natural emergent behavior in group scenes, no unrealistic synchronization
- **Physical interaction**: Characters pushing/pulling objects, falling into/onto surfaces, handling props realistically
- **Identity preservation**: Each subject maintains consistent appearance, proportions, behavior across interactions

**Edge Cases & Known Weakness Mitigation:**
- Minimize deformations at extreme camera angles or fast motion
- Eliminate edge-case motion issues (e.g., limb pop-in, disappearing objects, sudden scale shifts)
- Suppress high-frequency visual noise from generative artifacts
- Prevent audio distortion, clipping, or unnatural synthesis artifacts
- Eliminate lip-sync errors in multi-speaker, fast-talking, or overlapping dialogue scenes
- Handle multi-subject consistency failures (e.g., character duplication, omission, scale mismatch)
- Support text restoration in edits (text should remain legible and consistent)
- Eliminate overly conservative extension quality (produce bold, confident continuations)

## Technical Guidance for Implementation

### Architecture Overview (High-Level)

**Base Design Principles:**
- Unified large-scale efficient architecture for **joint multi-modal audio-video generation**
- Inspired by modern video foundations: latent diffusion/flow-matching or hybrid autoregressive + diffusion backbones
- Strong temporal modeling with transformer-based core (DiT/MMDiT-style with multi-modal conditioning)

**Component Design:**

1. **Encoder Layer**
   - **Text encoder**: Large language model backbone (e.g., LLaMA, GPT-style) for semantic understanding of complex narratives
   - **Image encoder**: Vision Transformer (ViT) or vision backbone for visual concept extraction
   - **Video encoder**: Spatiotemporal encoder (3D CNN, Video Vision Transformer, temporal transformer) for motion understanding
   - **Audio encoder**: Specialist encoder for speech, music, ambient (e.g., wav2vec2-style, conformer, or domain-specific speech/music encoders)

2. **Latent Space & Conditioning**
   - **Joint or aligned conditioning**: Either fused multimodal latent space or carefully aligned multi-stream conditioning
   - **Hierarchical conditioning**: Support both global (scene, overall style) and local (character, object) conditioning signals
   - **Temporal conditioning**: Explicit timeline/sequence modeling for multi-shot narratives and continuation

3. **Generation Backbone**
   - **Diffusion/Flow-matching core**: Latent video diffusion (LVD) or flow matching in learned latent space
   - **Multi-condition fusion**: Mechanism to blend text, image, video, audio conditioning without conflicts
   - **Temporal factorization**: Efficient temporal modeling to support 4–15 second outputs at scale

4. **Decoder Layer**
   - **Video decoder**: High-fidelity VAE or generative decoder to produce 480p/720p video
   - **Audio decoder**: Separate neural vocoder or audio synthesis module for high-quality audio output
   - **Multi-track assembly**: Mechanism to combine dialogue, music, ambient, foley into final binaural/multi-track output

### Training Strategy

**Pre-Training Phase:**
1. **Large-scale multimodal dataset** (100M+ video-audio pairs, curated for quality, diversity, realism)
   - Professional video/film footage for cinematography and motion realism
   - Diverse global content for linguistic/cultural/artistic diversity
   - High-quality audio (dialogue, music, ambient, foley, spatial audio)
   - Balanced representation across all supported task categories

2. **Contrastive multimodal alignment** (text-image-video-audio association learning)
   - Joint embedding space for all modalities
   - Cross-modal retrieval objectives to enforce semantic coherence

3. **Native audio-video generation training**
   - Joint denoising objectives for video and audio latent streams
   - Temporal coherence loss for frame-to-frame stability
   - Audio-visual synchronization loss (ensuring timing alignment)
   - Reconstruction losses on reference materials (image, video, audio)

### Post-Training & Alignment

1. **Reward Modeling**
   - Fine-grained rubrics across SeedVideoBench 2.0 categories (motion, prompt following, aesthetics, audio quality, AV sync, etc.)
   - Human preference data collection on representative samples
   - Build preference model for downstream RL alignment

2. **Reinforcement Learning (GRPO-style)**
   - Optimize generation policy using preference signals
   - Iterative improvement on weak categories (e.g., fine-grained instruction following, edge-case motion, audio expressiveness)
   - Balance improvement across all dimensions without catastrophic forgetting

3. **Instruction Following Fine-Tuning**
   - Synthetic data generation for complex multi-modal prompts
   - Stress-test scenarios (counter-reality, surreal, extreme physics, special art styles)
   - Few-shot adaptation to new task combinations

### Inference Optimization

1. **Standard Mode (High-Quality)**
   - Full model size, maximum quality
   - ~15–30 second generation time per 4–15 sec output (target <30s latency for interactive workflows)

2. **Fast Mode**
   - Model distillation or optimized sampling strategy
   - ~3–8 second generation time per output
   - Slight quality reduction acceptable for speed-critical workflows
   - Achieved via: fewer diffusion steps, lower latent resolution, quantization, or lightweight alternative architecture

3. **Controllability Features**
   - **Classifier-free guidance**: Enable/disable text, image, audio, video guidance independently
   - **Multi-condition fusion**: Fine-grained control over reference blending (e.g., 70% subject appearance from image, 30% from video)
   - **Negative prompting**: Support "avoid X" style instructions
   - **Seed control**: Reproducible generation from same seed
   - **Continuation control**: Fine-grained timeline positioning, blending parameters for seamless extension

4. **Metadata Output**
   - Generation parameters (seed, guidance scale, reference weights, etc.)
   - Consistency markers (subject tracking, motion vectors, audio timing offsets)
   - Confidence scores per region (useful for professional pipelines to identify areas needing manual polish)
